The Doctor and the Mister can't wait to see you in San Francisco.

The party will take place at the White Palace, a renovated old victorian house in central San Francisco.

What: An evening of revelry to celebrate our kick-ass partnership, made better and more complete by all of you.
Cocktails and dancing, with a little ceremonial interlude thrown in.
Black tie optional.

Where: 1045 Divisadero Street
San Francisco, California

Time: 7:30pm until the wee hours

Please RSVP on our site to let us know that you'll join us.

Other activities: For our out-of-town guests, we will be hosting a brunch on Sunday morning to spend a little more time with those who travelled from far away. More details on the brunch location and time will be posted here as we get closer to the event.


We prefer you gift us the pleasure of your attendance, but for those who have asked, we have a registry, listed under Sean-Michael or Samantha, at Heath Ceramics


We love San Francisco, which is why we've decided to celebrate here in our adopted home.

Airport information: SFO, the San Francisco International Airport, has many many flights in and out of town. BART leaves from SFO and will drop you off in San Francisco. Flights into Oakland (OAK) may be a little cheaper, but you will still need to take a shuttle from the airport to BART to get into the city.

Hotel information: We have reserved a block of rooms at the Hotel, but we also recommend looking into either VRBO or airbnb.com to find more homey accommodations. Neighborhoods near our event include: NOPA (or north of the panhandle), Lower Haight, Upper Haight, Fillmore, Castro, or Duboce Triangle.

We recommend against bringing a car, since we'd like you to raise a glass or two with us, and parking can be a little difficult in the residential neighborhood where we're holding the party.

You can walk, bike, bus, or cab to the party.
Taxi cabs include: Green Cab 415-626-GREEN; Luxor Cabs 415-282-4141; DeSoto Cab 415-970-1300; or Yellow Cab 415-333-3333.
